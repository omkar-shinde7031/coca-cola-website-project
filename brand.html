COCA COLA STORY

Refreshing the world, one story at a time. Since 1886, we've created happiness through delicious beverages.

OUR TIMELINE

1886

Dr. John Pemberton creates a distinctive flavored syrup in Atlanta, Georgia.

1915

The iconic contour bottle design introduced, becoming one of the most recognized packaging designs in history.

1928

Coca-Cola accompanies the Olympic Games to Amsterdam, marking the start of sports sponsorships.

1971

"I'd Like to Buy the World a Coke" commercial airs, becoming one of the most famous ads in history.

Today

Serving 1.9 billion drinks daily in more than 200 countries around the globe.
